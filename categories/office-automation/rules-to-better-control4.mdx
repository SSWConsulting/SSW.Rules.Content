[Control4](https://www.control4.com/) is a home and office automation platform that lets you manage lighting, climate, entertainment, and security from a single interface.

Its strength is simplicity. Instead of juggling multiple apps and remotes, everything works together in one system.

Done well, Control4 makes spaces more convenient, more comfortable, and more secure through reliable, integrated technology.
