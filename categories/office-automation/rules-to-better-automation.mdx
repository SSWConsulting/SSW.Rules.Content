Automation uses technology to reduce manual work and improve consistency. By combining software, systems, and smart devices, it increases efficiency, accuracy, and productivity across industries.

It is no longer limited to factories and large enterprises. Home and office automation now allow teams and individuals to streamline lighting, climate, security, and other everyday processes.

Done well, automation removes friction, reduces errors, and frees people to focus on higher-value work.
