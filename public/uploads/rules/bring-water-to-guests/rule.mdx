As someone welcoming people into your space, you set the tone from the moment they walk in.

One of the simplest ways to create a positive first impression is to always offer water 💧. If appropriate, offer coffee or tea as well.

<endIntro />

* It costs almost nothing
* It signals professionalism
* It makes people feel welcome

## Why this matters

Offering a drink is not about hydration. It is about hospitality.

* It shows you are thoughtful and prepared
* It makes your guest feel looked after
* It creates an immediate positive interaction
* It helps nervous candidates or clients settle in
* It gives them something to hold, which reduces awkwardness

That small gesture lowers tension and builds trust.

## The psychology behind it

When someone accepts a drink, they have already said “yes” once. You have created the first agreement.

That subtle cooperation makes the rest of the meeting feel smoother and more collaborative.
