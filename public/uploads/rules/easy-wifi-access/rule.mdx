When guests visit your office, make it easy for them to connect by providing Wi-Fi access via a QR code. It avoids the hassle of typing long passwords and gets them online in seconds.

<!--endintro-->

::: info
QR codes can easily be created with services like [QR Code Monkey](https://www.qrcode-monkey.com/#wifi).
:::
