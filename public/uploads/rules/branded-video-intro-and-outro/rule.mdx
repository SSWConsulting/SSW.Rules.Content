## Visual Graphic

A branded intro and outro will associate the video with your company. Intros are the visuals at the very beginning of your video, while outros appear in the end.

## Closing Music

Music is part of your branding too - just like your intro and outro graphics, viewers come to recognise the channel by its signature sign-off track. Start playing the closing music softly at the start of your last sentence, then let it swell as you sign off, so the video ends on a polished, recognisable note.
