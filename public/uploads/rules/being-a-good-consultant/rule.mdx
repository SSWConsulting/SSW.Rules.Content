The 3 A's for being a good consultant:

1. Having **Ability**
2. Being **Affable**
3. Being **Available**

<endIntro />

<youtubeEmbed url="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Rat-WopuWT4" description="Video: The 3 A's of Being a Good Consultant (1 min 23 sec)" />

Being available may be hard sometimes, but it doesn't mean replying instantly. As long as you can give a deadline and do it customers will be happy.
