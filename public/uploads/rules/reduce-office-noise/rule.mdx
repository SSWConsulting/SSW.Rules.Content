When the noise in an open plan office goes beyond appropriate levels, it can be annoying and stressful and thereby affects productivity. 

Here are a few tips to help reduce noise in your workplace:

<endIntro />

* [Use headphones](https://www.ssw.com.au/rules/use-a-headset/) 🎧
* Use a reasonable voice level or soft voice
* Should you anticipate a long call, move into a private room or booth if you can
  * Close your booth/pod door (if there is one) while you’re in calls
* During the conversation, [take notes of the important points](https://www.ssw.com.au/rules/do-you-know-you-should-write-notes-when-an-activity-is-going/) to avoid unnecessary repetition
