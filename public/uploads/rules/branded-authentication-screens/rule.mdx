Authentication confirmation screens are easy to overlook. After users sign in with an identity provider like Microsoft, Google, or GitHub, they'll often see a confirmation screen before being redirected back to your application.

Although users only see this screen for a few seconds, it's still part of your product. 

<endIntro />

To reassure users they're in the right place and create a smoother and consistent experience, add to the screen:

* Branding (logo and colours)
* A green tick to indicate success
* A link to your website

## Why brand these screens?

Branded authentication confirmation screens:

* Reinforce your company's identity
* Increase user confidence that the sign-in was successful
* Create a seamless experience between the authentication provider and your application
* Demonstrate attention to detail and professionalism
