- [Bubble Navigation](https://tympanus.net/Tutorials/BubbleNavigation/) (Hover each menu item to bubbling animation)

- [HTC Clock](https://www.jqueryscript.net/demo/HTC-Like-Weather-Clock-Plugin-jDigiClock)
