AI design tools are great for creating new UI quickly, but they can also help reduce design debt by reviewing the small details that make a product feel inconsistent or unfinished. 

These tools can be used to generate consistent UI, but their real value is not just in creating something new, it is in helping teams improve what already exists. 

Design debt builds up when small inconsistencies are shipped over time: uneven spacing, mismatched buttons, weak text hierarchy, misaligned icons, inconsistent component states, and awkward responsive layouts.
