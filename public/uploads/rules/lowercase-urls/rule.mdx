When creating URLs, **stick to lowercase letters**. Some servers and search engines treat URLs as case-sensitive, so uppercase and lowercase URLs are not interchangeable

<endIntro />

## Why uppercase in URLs is a bad idea

* **Case sensitivity** - Many servers (e.g., Linux) treat `/About` and `/about` as different URLs. Uppercases can cause duplicates or broken links
* **SEO risks** - Search engines like Google may treat them as separate pages, splitting ranking signals
* **User confusion** - People don’t remember casing, so mixed case makes URLs harder to share and type
* **Inconsistency** - Mixed casing looks messy and harder to scan
