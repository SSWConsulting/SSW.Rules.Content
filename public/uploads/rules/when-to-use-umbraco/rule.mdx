[Umbraco](https://umbraco.com) is a .NET-based CMS that works well for businesses already using the Microsoft ecosystem and needing a powerful, editor-friendly website platform. It is often chosen for enterprise websites where content editing, permissions, and governance are important.

<endIntro />

## Benefits of Umbraco

* Strong integration with Microsoft and .NET environments
* User-friendly content editing experience for non-technical teams
* Flexible architecture for custom website development
* Good support for permissions, workflows, and governance
* Reliable choice for enterprise and corporate websites
* Large ecosystem of developers, packages, and community support
