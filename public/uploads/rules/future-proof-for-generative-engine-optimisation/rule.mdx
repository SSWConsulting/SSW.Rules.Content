Search is rapidly evolving from a list of blue links into an AI-driven conversational experience. If your content is not optimised for generative engines like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Google’s AI Overviews, your brand may become invisible, even if you rank well in traditional search.

<endIntro />

### Visibility 

Generative engines prefer entities over pages. Without structured data, your brand is treated as just another webpage instead of a recognisable, authoritative source.

When a website lacks structured data and relies solely on unstructured HTML, it leaves AI systems guessing, making it difficult for them to verify the brand’s identity or establish its authority. In contrast, a site that proactively implements Organisation, FAQ, and Local Business schema provides a clear roadmap for generative engines. This technical clarity allows AI to accurately categorise the business and confidently present it as a trusted source to users.

#### Best practices:

* Use Schema Markup to define your brand as an entity
* Implement relevant schemas such as FAQ, How-To, and Local Business
* Keep structured data accurate, consistent, and up to date

### AI-Readable Content Formatting

Generative engines summarise and synthesise information. Content that is poorly structured or overly verbose is harder for large language models to interpret accurately.

#### Best practices:

* Write concise, clear explanations
* Provide contextual depth without unnecessary filler
* Use logical headings and structured sections
* Answer questions directly
* Use a clear structure as it benefits both human readers and LLMs 

### Community Mentions and Citations

Generative engines depend heavily on trusted third-party sources. If your brand is not mentioned in authoritative communities, it is less likely to appear in AI-generated answers.

#### Focus on: 

* Mentions in niche forums 
* Participation on Reddit and Quora 
* Citations from respected industry communities 
* Helpful, non-promotional contributions that naturally reference your brand 

### Summary

Generative Engine Optimisation is no longer optional. By optimising for AI systems through structured data, readable content, community presence, and emerging technical standards, you future-proof your brand’s visibility in the next generation of search.
