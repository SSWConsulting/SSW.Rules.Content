With 1.4 billion monthly active users, WeChat has evolved far beyond a social app into China's ultimate digital infrastructure.

This single platform hosts an entire self-contained ecosystem—combining social feeds, corporate communication, content channels, a payment system used 2.7 times daily per person, and nearly a billion users on Mini Programs.

It is a powerful, multi-product network built to convert traffic into transactions.

<imageEmbed alt="Image" size="large" showBorder={false} figurePrefix="none" figure="Leveraging the WeChat ecosystem to convert global traffic into transactions within the Chinese market" src="/uploads/rules/wechat-for-business/Wechat-1.png" />

<endIntro />

To navigate this massive ecosystem and successfully enter the Chinese market, you must understand its unique architecture. While WeChat restricts international entities from accessing certain product lines, you can fully leverage its core commercial features to drive growth and scale your business in China. Here is what you need to know.

## 1. WeChat (Personal) Account

A Personal WeChat Account is the ecosystem's foundational identity.

Think of it as your Personal Facebook Profile—but combined with an Apple ID & Stripe Master Admin.

We use a WeChat personal account to:

* Access instant messaging, group chats, and social sharing (Moments)
* Register, manage, link, and pay for all services related to the WeChat ecosystem

## 2. How to register a personal WeChat account (For International Mobile Numbers)

Registering for WeChat is just like setting up a WhatsApp account. It’s tied to your mobile number.

**Step 1: Download the App**

&#x9;Download WeChat from your local app store (e.g., App Store, Google Play)

**Step 2: Enter Details**

&#x9;Select your country, enter your mobile number, and set a password

<imageEmbed alt="Image" size="small" showBorder={false} figurePrefix="none" figure="Input your user name, country, mobile number and password" src="/uploads/rules/wechat-for-business/Wechat-register-1.png" />

**Step 3: SMS Activation**

Enter the verification code received via SMS

### ⚠️ Potential Security Verification

To prevent fraud, WeChat might prompt you with one of these security checks during registration:

<imageEmbed alt="Image" size="small" showBorder={false} figurePrefix="none" figure="WeChat security verification " src="/uploads/rules/wechat-for-business/Wechat-register-2.png" />

* **Friend Assistance** - An existing, identity-verified WeChat user must scan your registration QR code.
* **Bank Card Verification** - If no friend is available, link your credit or debit card (Visa, Mastercard, or JCB) and send a specific SMS to a designated gateway number to finalize activation.

### 💡 Crucial Anti-Fraud Tips

WeChat enforces strict fraud-prevention controls. To avoid instant account suspension:

* **Turn off all VPNs**

Do not use VPNs, proxies, or network accelerators during registration. Use your regular local 4G/5G data or standard Wi-Fi.

* **Keep your QR code private**

Never post your registration QR code on public platforms for strangers to scan.

* **Device Safety**

Ensure your phone is not jailbroken (iOS) or rooted (Android).

### 🌍 Registration Eligibility

❌ International Corporate Entities

✅ International Individuals

## 4. How to register WeCom (For Global Businesses)

### ⚠️ Before you start

Require real-name verified personal WeChat account (via passport or bank card). Acts as mandatory digital passport.

### Standard Flow (Without Verified Service Account)

**Step 1**

* Visit the [official WeCom Registration Page](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/) via your desktop browser
* Select WeCom

**Step 2**

* Input company name
* Choose industry
* Choose staff size

**Step 3**

* Input international mobile number
* Complete SMS verification

**Step 4**

* Scan QR code with the verified WeChat account
* Bind Administrator
* Accept Terms and Register done

**Step 5 Optional**

To unlock full CRM capabilities, you must verify your business by

* Submit your local business registration certificate
* Submit administrator's ID/Passport.
* Verification Fee $99 USD/year

### Fast-Track Flow (With Verified Service Account)

Bypass manual setup and $99 USD verification fee

**Step 1**

* Visit the [official WeCom Registration Page](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/)
* Log into verified WeChat Service Account

**Step 2**

* Left menu, click on ‘WeCom’

**Step 3**

* Select Register via Service Account authorization (Auto-syncs verified status & waives fees)

**Step 4**

* Enter Admin's Real Name, Mobile Number
* Input SMS code

**Step 5**

* Scan QR code with verified WeChat account
* Bind Administrator
* Creates a verified WeCom account instantly

## 5. WeChat vs WeCom - Key Differences

|              | WeChat                                 | WeCom (Enterprise WeChat)                           |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Purpose      | Private social networking              | Corporate collaboration & CRM                       |
| Cap Size     | 10,000 contacts                        | Unlimited                                           |
| Branding     | Informal personal nickname             | Verified corporate ID with official blue suffix     |
| Ownership    | Personal owned                         | Owned by company (supports client Inheritance)      |
| Post Moments | Manual posts (easily blocked by users) | Company-managed assets directly to client feeds     |
| Analytics    | None                                   | Full dashboard (tracking churn, growth, engagement) |
| Integration  | Strictly banned                        | Open APIs for global CRMs, Automation, & AI         |

## 6. What’s Service Accounts

Think of a WeChat Service Account as your company's official, verified mobile app operating inside the WeChat ecosystem (similar to the WhatsApp Business API with a customized menu).

It is perfectly suited for B2C, Retail, E-commerce, Banking, Logistics, and Airlines.

International companies can register using local business licenses.

<imageEmbed alt="Image" size="medium" showBorder={false} figurePrefix="none" figure="This is Aesop's WeChat Service Account. It not only links directly to their WeChat Mini-Program, but also offers a range of online features, such as a store locator, personal center, and booking center." src="/uploads/rules/wechat-for-business/Wechat-service1.png" />

### **Key Advantages**

* Advanced API Access: Supports template notifications (automated transactional updates like orders or bookings), integrates with WeChat Mini-program and other platforms such as Microsoft Dynamics 365
* Direct External Linking: Uniquely permitted to link menu buttons directly to your international corporate website or external portals.
* Ad Platform Ready: Fully eligible to open an Overseas Advertiser account to run WeChat Moments Ads through Tencent or authorized global partners.
* Monthly Broadcasts: Permits up to 4 rich-media broadcast bundles per month to all subscribers.

(Note: Integration with local WeChat Pay is restricted; it requires a specific cross-border merchant account)

### 🌏Registration Eligibility

✅ International Corporate Entities

❌ International Individuals

## 7. How to register Service Account (For Global Businesses)

### ⚠️ Before you start

Require real-name verified Personal WeChat account (via passport or bank card). Acts as mandatory digital passport.

**Step 1 Create Account Portal**

* Go to [WeChat Official Accounts Platform](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/)
* Click "Register Now"
* Select "Service Account" as your account type
* Input corporate email for admin login

**Step 2 Admin Binding & Submit Company**

* Scan QR code (using verified Personal WeChat account) to bind admin
* Select country/region
* Upload local business registration document
* Provide admin identity details (passport/local ID scan)

**Step 3 Complete Official Verification**

* Opt into annual WeChat Verification
* Pay $99 USD annual verification fee
* Await third-party audit contact (5–7 business days)
* Activate account

## 9. What’s WeChat Mini-Programs

Think of a WeChat Mini-Program as a web app that runs directly inside WeChat. Users can access your store or service in one click, without downloading any files or leaving the chat interface.

<imageEmbed alt="Image" size="large" showBorder={false} figurePrefix="none" figure="This is the login page for the Mini Program version of SugarLearning, SSW's onboarding system. It allows employees to easily complete the onboarding process directly through WeChat." src="/uploads/rules/wechat-for-business/WeChat-Mini-Program.png" />

### Key Advantages:

* No Installation: Runs instantly inside WeChat with no download required
* Lower Cost: Development costs are **60% lower** than standalone apps
* Ecosystem Closed-Loop: Connects WeChat tools (Service Accounts, Channels, WeCom, etc.) to convert traffic into instant transactions.
* Instant Deployment: Custom-build your own e-commerce stores, SaaS portals, booking systems, or membership hubs.

### 🌏Registration Eligibility

✅ International Corporate Entities

❌ International Individuals

## 10. How to register Mini-Program (For Global Businesses)

### ⚠️ Before you start

Require real-name verified Personal WeChat account (via passport or bank card). Acts as mandatory digital passport.

### Standard Flow (No Verified Service Account)

**Step 1 Create Account Portal**

* Go to the [WeChat Official Accounts Platform](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/)
* Click "Register Now" and select "Mini-Program"
* Input a corporate email

**Step 2 Admin Binding & Submit Information**

* Scan QR code (using verified Personal WeChat account) to bind admin
* Select country/region
* Upload local business registration documents
* Provide admin identity details (passport/local ID scan)

**Step 3 Complete Official Verification**

* Opt into the annual WeChat Verification process
* Pay the standard $99 USD annual fee
* Await third-party audit validation (5–7 business days)
* Activate developer permissions

### Fast-Track Flow (With a Verified Service Account)

Bypass the manual setup and the $99 USD verification fee

**Step 1 Access Integration Portal**

* Log into the verified Service Account admin dashboard
* Locate the "Mini-Program Management" module

**Step 2 Admin Authorization & Data Mirroring**

* Scan the authorization QR code (using the master admin's real-name verified personal account)
* Select the option to mirror entity verification data and operator info

**Step 3: Instant Activation**

* Input a new, distinct corporate email for the Mini-Program login
* Instantly generate a fully verified Mini-Program developer account

## 13. What are WeChat Moments Ads

WeChat’s native feed ads display seamlessly within users’ personal timelines, functioning exactly like Instagram or Facebook Feed Ads.

<imageEmbed alt="Image" size="medium" showBorder={false} figurePrefix="none" figure="Moments Ads" src="/uploads/rules/wechat-for-business/WeChat-ads.png" />

### Key Advantages

* **Precise Targeting:** Delivers highly accurate audience matching based on location, demographics, and behavior, directing users to Mini-Programs or landing pages with a single tap
* **No Local Entity Required:** Via the "International Advertiser" program, global brands can easily open accounts and settle ad spend in USD/HKD
* \*\*Frictionless Self-Serve: \*\*The most straightforward WeChat feature for a Western company to launch, manage, and scale independently

### Industry Restrictions

* **Allowed:** E-commerce (Retail/Fashion/Skin Care), Tourism (Hotels/Airlines), Consumer Electronics, and Luxury Goods.
* **Restricted:** Higher Education, Financial Services, and Real Estate (Requires manual white-list approval).
* **Prohibited:** Healthcare/Pharmaceuticals, Cryptocurrencies, Gambling, Legal Services, and K-12 Tutoring.

Also, all ad creatives must strictly comply with China's Advertising Law and WeChat's ecosystem safety codes.

### 🌏 Registration Eligibility

✅ International Corporate Entities (Via WeChat Pay Global / Cross-Border Account)

❌ International Individuals (Cannot register as a commercial merchant)
