With 1.4 billion monthly active users, WeChat has evolved far beyond a social app into China's ultimate digital infrastructure.

This single platform hosts an entire self-contained ecosystem—combining social feeds, corporate communication, content channels, a payment system used 2.7 times daily per person, and nearly a billion users on Mini Programs.

It is a powerful, multi-product network built to convert traffic into transactions.

Before diving into individual products, here's a quick overview of which parts of the WeChat ecosystem are available to international businesses and how they are typically used.

| Product                       | Best For                   | Eligible for Overseas? | Typical Use Cases                                                                                                              |
| ----------------------------- | -------------------------- | ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **WeChat (Personal) Account** | Admin identity             | ✅ Individual           | Register and manage ecosystem services                                                                                         |
| **WeChat Service Account**    | Official business presence | ✅ Company              | Bank transaction alerts, shipping notifications, appointment reminders, customer service menus, links to your official website |
| **WeChat Mini Program**       | Commerce & transactions    | ✅ Company              | Shopify-style stores, hotel booking apps, university portals, learning apps, event registration systems                        |
| **WeCom**                     | CRM & customer management  | ✅ Company              | Sales teams chatting with prospects, customer support agents, account managers maintaining client relationships                |
| **WeChat Moments Ads**        | Paid Acquisition           | ✅ Company              | Facebook/Instagram-style feed advertising to acquire customers                                                                 |
| WeChat Official Account       | Content publishing         | ❌ Company              | Company news, industry articles, thought leadership content, product updates, employer branding                                |
| WeChat Store                  | Native commerce            | ❌ Company              | Selling products directly inside WeChat without building a custom e-commerce app                                               |
| Livestream Commerce           | Live selling               | ❌ Company              | Product launches, influencer-led sales events, live product demonstrations, flash-sale campaigns                               |

For most overseas companies, the most practical and accessible path into the WeChat ecosystem is:

**WeChat (Personal) Account → Service Account → Mini-Program → WeCom → Moments Ads**

This combination provides an official brand presence, a lightweight transactional platform, customer relationship management capabilities, and scalable advertising access.

While WeChat restricts international entities from accessing certain product lines, these four products form the core commercial stack that most global businesses can successfully deploy in China.

Here is what you need to know.

## 1. WeChat (Personal) Account

A Personal WeChat Account is the ecosystem's foundational identity.

Think of it as your Personal Facebook Profile—but combined with an Apple ID & Stripe Master Admin.

We use a WeChat personal account to:

* **Communicate** - Access instant messaging, group chats, and social sharing (Moments)
* **Admin** - Register, manage, link, and pay for all services related to the WeChat ecosystem

## 2. How to register a personal WeChat account (For International Mobile Numbers)

Registering for WeChat is just like setting up a WhatsApp account. It’s tied to your mobile number.

1. **Download the App** - Download WeChat from your local app store (e.g., App Store, Google Play)
2. **Enter Details** - Select your country, enter your mobile number, and set a password
3. **SMS Activation** - Enter the verification code received via SMS

<imageEmbed alt="Image" size="small" showBorder={false} figurePrefix="none" figure="Input your user name, country, mobile number and password" src="/uploads/rules/wechat-for-business/Wechat-register-1.png" />

### ⚠️ Potential Security Verification

To prevent fraud, WeChat might prompt you with one of these security checks during registration:

<imageEmbed alt="Image" size="small" showBorder={false} figurePrefix="none" figure="WeChat security verification " src="/uploads/rules/wechat-for-business/Wechat-register-2.png" />

* **Friend Assistance** - An existing, identity-verified WeChat user must scan your registration QR code
* **Bank Card Verification** - If no friend is available, link your credit or debit card (Visa, Mastercard, or JCB) and send a specific SMS to a designated gateway number to finalize activation

### 💡 Crucial Anti-Fraud Tips

WeChat enforces strict fraud-prevention controls. To avoid instant account suspension:

**Turn off all VPNs** - Do not use VPNs, proxies, or network accelerators during registration. Use your regular local 4G/5G data or standard Wi-Fi

**Keep your QR code private** - Never post your registration QR code on public platforms for strangers to scan

**Device Safety** - Ensure your phone is not jailbroken (iOS) or rooted (Android)

### 🌍 Who can register

❌ Overseas Companies

✅ Foreign Individuals

## 3. What’s a Service Account

Think of a WeChat Service Account as your company's official, verified mobile app operating inside the WeChat ecosystem (similar to the WhatsApp Business API with a customized menu).

It is perfectly suited for B2C, Retail, E-commerce, Banking, Logistics, and Airlines.

International companies can register using local business licenses.

<imageEmbed alt="Image" size="medium" showBorder={false} figurePrefix="none" figure="This is Aesop's WeChat Service Account. It not only links directly to their WeChat Mini-Program, but also offers a range of online features, such as a store locator, personal center, and booking center" src="/uploads/rules/wechat-for-business/Wechat-service1.png" />

### Key Advantages

* **Powerful API Access** - Send automated, one-to-one notifications (order confirmations, booking reminders, payment updates) that don't count against your monthly broadcast quota. The API also connects your account to Mini-programs, CRMs, and systems like Microsoft Dynamics 365
* **Direct External Linking** - The custom bottom menu can link directly to your company website or external portals — something most other account types can't do
* **Eligible to run ads - **Overseas companies can open an Overseas Advertiser account and run WeChat Moments ads, either through Tencent directly or an authorized agency
* **Up to 4 broadcasts per month** - You can mass-send up to 4 rich-media (article-style) posts to all your followers each month. This is your content/marketing channel — separate from the one-to-one notifications above, so don't confuse the two

1. **Create Account Portal** - Go to the [WeChat Official Accounts Platform](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/?lang=en_US), click "Register Now", select "Service Account" as your account type, and input your company email for admin login
2. **Bind Admin - **Scan the QR code using a verified personal WeChat account to bind the admin, select your country/region, upload your local business registration document, and provide the admin's identity details (passport/local ID scan)

## 5. What’s WeChat Mini-Program

Think of a WeChat Mini-Program as a web app that runs directly inside WeChat. Users can access your store or service in one click, without downloading any files or leaving the chat interface.

<imageEmbed alt="Image" size="large" showBorder={false} figurePrefix="none" figure="This is the login page for the Mini Program version of SugarLearning, SSW's onboarding system. It allows employees to easily complete the onboarding process directly through WeChat" src="/uploads/rules/wechat-for-business/WeChat-Mini-Program.png" />

### Key Advantages

* **No download needed** — Opens instantly inside WeChat — users don't have to install anything from an app store
* **Cheaper to build** — Development typically costs around 60% less than building a standalone app
* **Plugs into the WeChat ecosystem** — Works hand-in-hand with your other WeChat tools (Service Account, Channels, WeCom, etc.), so you can turn followers into paying customers without sending them elsewhere
* **Build almost anything** — Use it to run your own online store, SaaS portal, booking system, or membership hub

### 🌍 Who can register

✅ Overseas Companies

❌ Foreign Individuals

### Standard Flow (No Verified Service Account yet)

1. **Create your account** - Go to the [WeChat Official Accounts Platform](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/?lang=en_US), click "Register Now", choose "Mini-Program", and sign up with a corporate email
2. **Bind an admin & submit your details** - Scan the QR code with a verified personal WeChat account to set the admin, pick your country/region, upload your business registration documents, and provide the admin's ID (passport or local ID scan)
3. **Complete verification** - Sign up for WeChat's annual verification and pay the US$99 yearly fee. A third-party auditor will check your details (5–7 business days), and your developer access goes live once approved

### Fast-Track Flow (With a Verified Service Account)

Bypass the manual setup and the $99 USD verification fee

1. **Open the integration panel** - Log into your verified Service Account dashboard and find the "Mini-Program Management" section
2. **Authorize & reuse your existing details** - Scan the authorization QR code with the master admin's real-name verified personal account, then choose to copy over your existing company verification and operator info
3. **Activate - **Enter a new, separate corporate email for the Mini-Program login, and a fully verified Mini-Program developer account is created instantly

### Standard Flow (Without Verified Service Account)

1. \*\*Visit the Website - \*\*Visit the [official WeCom Registration Page](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/) via your desktop browser and select WeCom
2. \*\*Enter Company Details - \*\*Input your company name, choose your industry and staff size
3. \*\*SMS Verification - \*\*Input your international mobile number and complete the SMS verification process
4. \*\*Bind Administrator - \*\*Scan the QR code with your verified WeChat account, bind the administrator, and accept the terms to complete registration
5. \*\*Verify Business (Optional) - \*\*Submit your local business registration certificate and the administrator's ID/Passport to unlock full CRM capabilities ($99 USD/year verification fee)

### Fast-Track Flow (With Verified Service Account)

Bypass manual setup and $99 USD verification fee

1. **Log In** - Visit the [official WeCom Registration Page](https://mp.weixin.qq.com/) and log into your verified WeChat Service Account
2. **Navigate Menu** - Click on ‘WeCom’ in the left menu
3. **Select Registration Method** - Choose to register via Service Account authorization (this auto-syncs your verified status and waives fees)
4. **Admin Details** - Enter the Administrator's real name and mobile number, then input the SMS verification code
5. **Bind Administrator** - Scan the QR code with your verified WeChat account to bind the Administrator and instantly create a verified WeCom account

## 9. WeChat vs WeCom - Key Differences

|              | WeChat                                 | WeCom (Enterprise WeChat)                           |
| ------------ | -------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Purpose      | Private social networking              | Corporate collaboration & CRM                       |
| Cap Size     | 10,000 contacts                        | Unlimited                                           |
| Branding     | Informal personal nickname             | Verified corporate ID with official blue suffix     |
| Ownership    | Personal owned                         | Owned by company (supports client Inheritance)      |
| Post Moments | Manual posts (easily blocked by users) | Company-managed assets directly to client feeds     |
| Analytics    | None                                   | Full dashboard (tracking churn, growth, engagement) |
| Integration  | Strictly banned                        | Open APIs for global CRMs, Automation, & AI         |

## 10. What are WeChat Moments Ads

WeChat’s native feed ads display seamlessly within users’ personal timelines, functioning exactly like Instagram or Facebook Feed Ads.

<imageEmbed alt="Image" size="medium" showBorder={false} figurePrefix="none" figure="Moments Ads" src="/uploads/rules/wechat-for-business/WeChat-ads.png" />

### Key Advantages

* **Precise Targeting -** Delivers highly accurate audience matching based on location, demographics, and behavior, directing users to Mini-Programs or landing pages with a single tap
* **No Local Entity Required -** Via the "International Advertiser" program, global brands can easily open accounts and settle ad spend in USD/HKD
* \*\*Frictionless Self-Serve - \*\*The most straightforward WeChat feature for a Western company to launch, manage, and scale independently

### Industry Restrictions

* **Allowed -** E-commerce (Retail/Fashion/Skin Care), Tourism (Hotels/Airlines), Consumer Electronics, and Luxury Goods
* **Restricted -** Higher Education, Financial Services, and Real Estate (Requires manual white-list approval)
* **Prohibited** - Healthcare/Pharmaceuticals, Cryptocurrencies, Gambling, Legal Services, and K-12 Tutoring

Also, all ad creatives must strictly comply with China's Advertising Law and WeChat's ecosystem safety codes

### 🌏Registration Eligibility

✅ International Corporate Entities (Via WeChat Pay Global / Cross-Border Account)

❌ International Individuals (Cannot register as a commercial merchant)

## 11. Other WeChat Products (Limited or Unavailable for International Companies)

Other WeChat products exist, but most overseas companies either cannot access them directly or have limited commercial value compared to the core stack above.

Here they are.

### WeChat Official Account

The public content distribution and brand media hub within WeChat ecosystem, focusing on broadcasting information.

Best for content-driven sectors (B2B, Consulting, SaaS, Media) to publish corporate news, industry insights, or technical manuals.

### WeChat Store

### WeChat Livestream Commerce

Livestream commerce attaches Mini Program / WeChat Store product links to a channel's livestream.

## 12. Key Takeaways

WeChat should be viewed as a business ecosystem rather than simply a social media platform.

For most international companies, the most practical approach is to start with a verified Service Account, add a Mini-Program when transactions are needed, use WeCom for customer relationship management, and leverage Moments Ads for growth and acquisition.

While some WeChat products remain unavailable to overseas entities, the core commercial tools are already accessible and sufficient for most businesses looking to establish a presence in the Chinese market.
