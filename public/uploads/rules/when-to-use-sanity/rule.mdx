[Sanity](https://www.sanity.io) is a headless CMS built for teams that want highly customizable content workflows and real-time collaboration. It is a strong choice when developers need full control over content structure and editors need a flexible, modern editing experience.

<endIntro />

## Benefits of Sanity

* Fully customizable content studio for tailored editor experiences
* Real-time collaboration for teams working on content together
* Strong developer experience with schema-based content models
* API-first architecture for websites, apps, and other platforms
* Flexible enough for both simple websites and complex digital products
* Great support for structured content and reusable components
