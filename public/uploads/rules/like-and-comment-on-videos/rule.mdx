Engaging with relevant content by liking and commenting on social media not only boosts their performance but also benefits creators and viewers. 

<endIntro />

## For creators

Engagement signals like likes and comments help platforms promote content to a wider audience. Encouraging interaction increases reach, visibility, and the chances your content will be discovered.

This is especially valuable for [test-related](/conduct-a-test-please) content.

If you're a video creator, encourage testers to like and comment on videos to confirm they've watched and to gather their valuable feedback. These interactions help build a history, like who are the viewers and their thoughts.

## For viewers and readers

Liking and commenting on valuable content helps others discover it and shows colleagues and clients the topics you care about. 

When relevant, include a link to your work to promote it in a natural and helpful way.
