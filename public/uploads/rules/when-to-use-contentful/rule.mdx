[Contentful](https://www.contentful.com) is a headless CMS designed for teams that need flexible content management across multiple channels like websites, apps, digital signage, and ecommerce platforms. It works well when content needs to be structured once and published everywhere, while giving developers full control over how the frontend is built.

<endIntro />

## Benefits of Contentful

* Structured content that can be reused across multiple platforms
* Strong API-first approach for modern frontend frameworks
* Scales well for large teams and enterprise environments
* Flexible content modeling for complex websites and applications
* Supports localization and multi-language content management
* Clear separation between content management and presentation
