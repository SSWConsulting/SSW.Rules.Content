For maximum readability and SEO use kebab-case (dashes) in your URLs and [make them short and friendly](/create-friendly-short-urls).

Learn more on [Best Practices for URL Structure](https://www.searchenginejournal.com/technical-seo/url-structure/#bestprac).

<endIntro />

<boxEmbed
  style="greybox"
  body={<>
    northwind.com/**pageonworddocumentation**
  </>}
  figurePrefix="bad"
  figure="No separation between words"
/>

<boxEmbed
  style="greybox"
  body={<>
    northwind.com/**PageOnWordDocumentation**
  </>}
  figurePrefix="bad"
  figure="PascalCase - Better readability and still works in small caps, so other people might share it without the MixedCase"
/>

<boxEmbed
  style="greybox"
  body={<>
    northwind.com/**page on word documentation**
    
    ...will become
    
    northwind.com/**page20%on20%word20%documentation**
  </>}
  figurePrefix="bad"
  figure="Spaces will show up in your URL as %20, which is bad for readability and SEO"
/>

<boxEmbed
  style="greybox"
  body={<>
    northwind.com/**page\_on\_word\_documentation**
  </>}
  figurePrefix="ok"
  figure="Underscored (snake_case) - Good readability but not recommended by Google"
/>

<boxEmbed
  style="greybox"
  body={<>
    northwind.com/**page-on-word-documentation**
  </>}
  figurePrefix="good"
  figure="Kebab-case - Recommended by Google. You should use lowercase only"
/>

<boxEmbed
  style="warning"
  body={<>
    **Not for domains!** This applies only to pages and documents. Domains with dashes are harder to read and less trustworthy
  </>}
  figurePrefix="none"
  figure=""
/>

Read more on [SEO 101: Hyphens vs. Underscores in URLs](https://www.seomechanic.com/seo-101-hyphens-underscores-_-urls/).
