Initializing the Project - See how to get the work items into Azure DevOps via Excel:

<endOfIntro />

- Open SSW Priorities Estimate Template in Excel
  
<imageEmbed
  alt="Image"
  size="large"
  showBorder={false}
  captionStyle="default"
  captionText={"Find SSW Template - 'New | My templates... | SSW_Proposal-Project | SSWPrioritiesEstimateTemplate.xltx''"}
  src="/uploads/rules/get-work-items-via-excel/ExcelSSWTemplate.jpeg"
/>


- Add the tasks and additional tasks into Release_XX sheet
  
<imageEmbed
  alt="Image"
  size="large"
  showBorder={false}
  captionStyle="default"
  captionText={"Copy work items to Release_XX sheet"}
  src="/uploads/rules/get-work-items-via-excel/ExcelReleaseSheet.jpeg"
/>


**Note:** The Project Management tasks will be calculated based on your tasks

- Create a new sheet and go to Team tab
- Click "New List" and choose your project
  
<imageEmbed
  alt="Image"
  size="large"
  showBorder={false}
  captionStyle="default"
  captionText={"Find your project in the list"}
  src="/uploads/rules/get-work-items-via-excel/ExcelFindProject.jpeg"
/>


  
<imageEmbed
  alt="Image"
  size="large"
  showBorder={false}
  captionStyle="default"
  captionText={"Choose 'Input list' to download the empty template"}
  src="/uploads/rules/get-work-items-via-excel/ExcelInputList.jpeg"
/>


- Add the other useful columns e.g. Baseline Work, Remaining Work, Completed Work into Excel
  
<imageEmbed
  alt="Image"
  size="large"
  showBorder={false}
  captionStyle="default"
  captionText={"Click 'Choose columns'"}
  src="/uploads/rules/get-work-items-via-excel/ExcelChooseColumnsButton.jpeg"
/>


  
<imageEmbed
  alt="Image"
  size="large"
  showBorder={false}
  captionStyle="default"
  captionText={"Select columns in the left list"}
  src="/uploads/rules/get-work-items-via-excel/ExcelChooseColumnsList.jpeg"
/>


- Copy work items (including the Project Management tasks) from Release_XX to the new sheet
  
<imageEmbed
  alt="Image"
  size="large"
  showBorder={false}
  captionStyle="default"
  captionText={"Copy work items from Release_XX"}
  src="/uploads/rules/get-work-items-via-excel/ExcelWorkItems.jpeg"
/>


- Click "Publish" button to upload the work items to TFS
