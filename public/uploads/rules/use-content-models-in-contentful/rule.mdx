In Contentful, poor content modelling quickly creates long-term problems.

Many teams create large generic content types that are difficult to scale, difficult to query, and confusing for editors.

<endIntro />

## Why this matters

Well-structured content models improve editorial clarity, API performance, and frontend flexibility.

They also reduce duplication and make content governance much easier.

## Common uses

* Separate content types for Articles, Authors, and Categories
* Reusable components for CTAs and Hero sections
* Shared SEO fields instead of repeating metadata everywhere
