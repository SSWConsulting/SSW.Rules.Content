The [Hemmingway Editor](https://hemingwayapp.com/) is a tool that analyzes content and highlights problematic areas. This process reveals common writing issues that impact readers. Use this tool regularly to improve clarity and readability.

<endIntro />

## Why use Hemmingway?

This is a tool that analyzes text for readability. It's particularly useful for longer pieces, like articles or blog posts.

The editor also reports a **grade-level readability score**.

| Grade Level | Readability        |
|------------|-------------------|
| Grade 12   | Harder to read     |
| Grade 9    | Easier to read     |
| Grade 7    | Even easier to read |

## How to use it

Copy and paste your content into the editor to receive instant feedback. Address the highlighted issues and aim for a Grade 9 readability score or lower.
