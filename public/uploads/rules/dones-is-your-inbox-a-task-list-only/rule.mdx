Most people don’t manage their inbox effectively. Emails should be treated as a list to do. As you complete a task you can just delete the email.

<endIntro />

Your inbox should **only** contain actionable items. So everything in your Inbox, including subfolders, should be [tasks](/dones-do-you-use-email-for-tasks-only-not-communication) that are actionable items on your to-do list.

So do it now, delete all emails you have done and the ones that aren't tasks.

<boxEmbed
  body={<>
    **Note:** If you want to keep an email that is not an actionable task (e.g. for future reference), move it to a folder outside your inbox (e.g. "Saved items")
    
    The same applies to email tasks assigned to others. If you need to follow up, store them on a separate folder (e.g. Named "Waiting on others")
  </>}
