These are the essential fields for timesheets:

<endIntro />

1. Client ID or Client Name
2. Location
3. Project and Iteration (if applicable)
4. Category (what kind of work it is)
5. Amount of time including Break (Minimum 1/2 hour break if you work more than 5.5 hours)
7. Notes - [Describe the things you've worked on](/how-to-describe-the-work-you-have-done), including people who you were working with - both internal or external (e.g. *"Worked with Eddie"*)
