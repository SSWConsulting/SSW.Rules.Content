A common mistake in [Sitefinity](https://www.progress.com/sitefinity-cms) is treating every page as a standard page instead of using dedicated content types.

When content such as News, Events, Resources, Team Members, or Case Studies has its own purpose, it should be managed as structured content rather than built manually inside page blocks.

<endIntro />

## Why this matters

Using proper content types improves search, filtering, reuse, permissions, and multilingual support.

It also makes personalization and marketing automation much easier to manage.

## Common uses

* News managed separately from landing pages
* Events with their own archive and date fields
* Team Members reused across multiple sections
* Resources and downloads grouped by category
