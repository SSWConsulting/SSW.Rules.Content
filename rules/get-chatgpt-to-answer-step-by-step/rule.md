Asking ChatGPT to think through its answer step by step can help improve the logic and coherence of its responses. By encouraging the model to break down complex topics or tasks into smaller components, you can ensure a more structured and well-reasoned output.
            
<!--endintro-->

Here are some tips for getting ChatGPT to think through its answer step by step:
Request that ChatGPT provide a step-by-step explanation or breakdown of its response.
* Encourage the model to consider potential issues or questions that may arise at each step
* Use a numbered or bullet-point format to guide the model in structuring its response
 
::: greybox
"ChatGPT, explain the process of cellular respiration step by step, highlighting the key stages and the role of each component involved."
:::
