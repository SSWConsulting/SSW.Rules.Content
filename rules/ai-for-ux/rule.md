### Best practices when using AI for UX

* **Validate output** ‚Äì AI can hallucinate. Always review and test anything before going live.
* **Use as a collaborator, not a crutch** ‚Äì AI should support your creativity, not replace your judgment.
* **Prompt wisely** ‚Äì The better your input, the better the output. Be specific and provide context.
* **Respect privacy** ‚Äì Avoid feeding sensitive data into AI tools that don't guarantee security.
