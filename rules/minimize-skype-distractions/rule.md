Skype is a great way to get distracted. Following these options helps you to minimize messaging/communication​ distractions:
<br><excerpt class='endintro'></excerpt><br>
<ol><li>Set your status to Busy - people will think twice before messaging you<br></li><li>Don't display an alert when a contact comes online or goes offline<br></li><li>​Don't play a sound when you receive an email.
