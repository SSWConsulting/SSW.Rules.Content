<span class='intro'> <ul><li>Be honest</li><li>Record your time whenever you are doing work for a client, even if it is to be written off.</li><li>Round start/end/break times to the nearest 10 minutes (avoid rounding up time as this gives the impression to the client we are &quot;penny pinching&quot;)</li><li>If you are working with another employee, ensure your times are consistent</li><li>If you are providing telephone support, count your time in 10 minute blocks</li></ul> </span>
