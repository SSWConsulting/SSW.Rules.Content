Background noise during Teams calls can be unprofessional and distracting for others.

This is a problem you can solve very easily! Here's how:

<!--endintro-->

## How to enable voice isolation

Voice isolation uses AI to mute background noise while keeping your voice clear.

Switch it on in *Settings | Devices | Noise Suppression | Enable Voice Isolation*

![Figure: How to enable voice isolation](settings.png)

::: good
![Figure: This icon means Voice Isolation is on](enabled.png)
:::

No more worrying about your noisy neighbours!
