<span class='intro'> <p>​Just as barcodes allow machines to read data from items in stores, QR codes are a barcode-like vector between online and offline information. QR codes can be easily accessed by any phone in the world that has a camera. They enable everything from online to offline (O2O) marketplaces, which are huge in China.<br></p> </span>

<h3 class="ssw15-rteElement-H3">#1&#160;Use a QR code to follow a WeChat Business/Brand Account<br></h3><p>Customers - WeChat allows users to follow a Business/Brand account upon scanning (use the built-in QR scanner). QR codes aren’t just used online, but also in a store or on business cards to save you typing in a URL.</p><p>Businesses - Followers of the WeChat Business/Brand accounts can be sent a limited number of messages per month, usually product info and promotions. <br></p><dl class="ssw15-rteElement-ImageArea"> <img src="/PublishingImages/Microsoft%20official%20account.GIF" alt="" style="width&#58;770px;" /> </dl><p>

   <strong>Figure</strong><strong>&#58; The WeChat app can scan the QR code, then allow the customer to click the follow button. They will then&#160;receive regular updates.<br>​</strong></p><h3 class="ssw15-rteElement-H3">#2 Use a QR code to share WeChat contact information</h3><p class="ssw15-rteElement-P">All WeChat users get their own customized QR code. It can be shown for anyone else to scan, or shared.</p><p class="ssw15-rteElement-P">Note&#58; QR codes can be scanned by tapping and hold on it on screen. <br></p><dl class="ssw15-rteElement-ImageArea"> <img src="/PublishingImages/personal%20qr%20code.png" alt="" style="width&#58;770px;" />  </dl><p class="ssw15-rteElement-P">

   <strong>Figure&#58; Tap and hold on a QR image to extract a QR code</strong><br>​<br></p><h3 class="ssw15-rteElement-H3">#3 Use a QR code to receive money in Alipay <br></h3><p>Alipay allows users to receive funds via QR code without adding the payee as a contact.<br>Users create a QR code for payer to scan and pay.<br></p><dl class="ssw15-rteElement-ImageArea"> <img src="/PublishingImages/receive%20money%20alipay.png" alt="" style="width&#58;660px;height&#58;628px;" /> </dl><p>

</p><p>Some social networks can also offer login by scanning a QR code using the network’s own app instead of entering a user name and a password.<br></p><dl class="ssw15-rteElement-ImageArea"> <img src="/PublishingImages/weibo%20login.png" alt="" style="width&#58;770px;height&#58;361px;" /> </dl> <strong>Figure&#58; use Weibo App to scan a QR code to login their services on the web.</strong><br>​<br>
<p></p><p></p><h3 class="ssw15-rteElement-H3">#8&#160;Use a QR code to connect a hotspot</h3><p>Wi-Fi via WeChat is a feature for businesses and public places offering free Wi-Fi hotspots to their customers. It’s a common service in China.&#160;Rather than having to find the publicly posted Wi-Fi password, users can log in quickly using their WeChat accounts.<br></p><dl class="ssw15-rteElement-ImageArea"> <img src="/PublishingImages/hotspot%20qr%20code.jpg" alt="" style="width&#58;770px;height&#58;787px;" /> </dl> <strong>Figure&#58; how to connect to a public hotspot</strong><br>​<br>
