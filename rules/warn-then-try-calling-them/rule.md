If it’s not urgent, before calling ping someone on IM first with "can I call you now?", because they might be with a client, or in a session.

If it is urgent or you are blocked, then proceed with a warning “Calling you in 5 mins to talk about xxx”.

<!--endintro-->
Even better, mention the reason before calling.

::: greybox
**Bob Northwind:** Calling in 5 minutes about TimePro.
:::
::: ok
OK example - The reason is mentioned.
:::

::: greybox
**Bob Northwind:** Calling in 5 minutes. See the PBI: [URL]

or

**Bob Northwind:** Calling in 5 minutes. See email subject: [Email subject]
:::
::: good
Good example - The reason is mentioned and a source is provided.
:::
