No matter how well adapted your business and processes are, there's always room to improve and learn. Once you've built a fantastic team of experts, it's important to actively listen to their opinions and ideas to continue building a great place to work and deliver more value to your customers.

<!--endintro-->

In the highly dynamic space of software development, there's new frameworks, features and languages constantly being released, hyped, maturing and sunsetting. No-one has the time to keep up with everything that's going on, so it makes sense to gauge the opionions of your team - there's a good chance that at least one of them has an opinion!
