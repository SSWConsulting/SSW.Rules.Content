<p class="ssw15-rteElement-P">​"Gamification" is a method of encouraging user participation. Usually, these are a set of incentives such as points or achievement badges which are linked to some other form of redeemable value.​​<br></p>
<br><excerpt class='endintro'></excerpt><br>
