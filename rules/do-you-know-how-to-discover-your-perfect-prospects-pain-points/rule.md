Before you move onto this step, first have your target audience information right next to you. Understanding your prospects most pressing pain points should be huge priority. <div>Start thinking - what do they want? What are their frustrations? What are their aspirations? And finally, what do they fear? Write down all the ideas you might come up with.<br></div><br>
