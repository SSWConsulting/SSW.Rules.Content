<p>​A great way of collaborating a team or presenting a new solution to a client is using a visual display of&#160;your recommendations. Using a&#160;whiteboard is a great way of collaborating and brainstorming which&#160;works quite well. But a physical&#160;whiteboard is a large ugly object that takes up a fair amount of room, often isn’t kept clean, and doesn’t allow for people to collaborate remotely. There are modern alternatives that work even better, let's have a look at them.&#160;<br></p><p>Software consultants often use architecture diagrams to present a solution to the client. These&#160;graphical representations are used to&#160;help all of the stakeholders, the developers, the designers and the clients visualise the high-level&#160;structure of the system&#160;or application&#160;to ensure it covers all of the client requirements.&#160;<br></p><dl class="image"><dt>
